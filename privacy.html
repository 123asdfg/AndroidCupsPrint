Privacy Policy

This app collects crash data and send them to Crashlytics.

There might be some sensitive data. Please refer to their privacy policy.

Crash data do NOT contain actual printed documents. These never leave your phone (except for your printer, of course).

This app will NEVER, EVER use the data sent to Crashlytics for ANY OTHER USE than troubleshooting. It will NEVER, EVER be shared to ANYONE for ANYTHING. I mean it, really, nothing will be done to that data except the help it provides to the developer.

If you don't want that, please compile the app yourself without this, or please use the f-droid version.
